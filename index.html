Aim Trainer

Aim Trainer is a game that helps you improve your mouse aiming accuracy. It features three different modes - Survival, Casual, and Speed - each with their own unique challenges. In Survival mode, you have to shoot down as many targets as you can with three lives. In Casual mode, you have to hit as many targets as possible within a limited time frame. In Speed mode, you have to click as quickly as possible within a five-second time limit.

Calculator

This calculator app is easy to use and can perform basic arithmetic operations, as well as calculate square roots, handle floating and negative numbers, and compute factorials. It saves your previous results for easy reference and has light and dark mode options. It can display up to 12 characters, making it suitable for both simple and complex calculations.

Alarm

My Android alarm app is a simple yet functional tool that lets you set multiple alarms, turn them off and on, or delete them as needed. It also has an alarm history feature that lets you restore any accidentally deleted alarms. But what makes my app unique is its innovative feature that requires you to solve equations before the alarm turns off. This helps you start your day off on the right foot and ensure that you wake up both your body and mind.
